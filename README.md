# Car Workshop & Spare Parts Management System
## MVP Business Requirements Document (BRD)

---

# 1. Project Overview

## Project Name
Car Workshop & Spare Parts ERP System

---

## Objective
Build a modular backend-first ERP-style system for:

- Car workshop operations
- Spare parts inventory management
- Purchasing
- Sales and invoicing
- Supplier management
- Customer and vehicle management

The system must support:

- Dynamic product specifications
- Inventory tracking
- Workshop part consumption
- Sales workflows
- Purchasing workflows
- Auditability
- Scalable domain architecture

---

# 2. Technical Architecture

## Backend Stack

| Area | Technology |
